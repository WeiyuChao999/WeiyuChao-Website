Creative Writing✒✏️️

My name is Weiyu Chao. I was born in 1999 in Yifeng County, Yichun City, Jiangxi Province. I am a quiet, funny, and shy girl. I enjoy the sunny day and imagination, that will make me feel relaxed. I like all the colors because I think every color has its own charm, so I won't spend time picking things. My hobby is playing computer games, writing stories, reading books, eating, going for a walk, and sleeping. Cats are my favorite pet and I hope I can have a Ragdoll in the future.

I have been studying at Arroyo Pacific Academy for the half-year and Arrowhead Christian Academy for four years. During these years, I got to know a lot of people, like my host parents and teachers. They gave me tremendous help in improving my English. In the beginning, my English was poor, some people laughed at me and I felt disappointed. I was so glad that people always give hands to me when I need help.

In March 2019. I received the offer from the University of California, Riverside to major in Creative Writing. Reading and writing have always been my hobby, and now I am fortunate enough to get a chance to pursue my passion. Creative writing uses imagination and creativity to create own stories, that's really the way that I want to use in my own stories. I am so happy for picking this major. I am looking forward to learning writing skills systematically as well as to get more inspirations and improvements in UCR UCR has a rich multi-cultural environment, which I am also looking forward to fully experience and being inspired upon.
